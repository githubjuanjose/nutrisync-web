NutriSync — Business Requirements by User Workflow

Purpose: a Product-Owner-verifiable list of business requirements for every user workflow, each with its intent (the "why"), and a check that web + apps deliver the nine capabilities from slide 7 of the deck.

How to use: POs review each requirement, confirm intent is right, and mark the Verify column (✅ met / ⚠️ partial / ❌ missing) per surface. The companion business-requirements.csv is the sign-off tracker.

Surfaces: M = mobile app (iOS/Android), W = web app, Mk = marketing/entry, Ad = admin/MIS.

Slide-7 capability codes (referenced as "Intent → [C#]")

  • C1 Science-backed cycle-sync framework
  • C2 Ethical data & privacy design
  • C3 Integrated cycle, nutrition & fitness plans
  • C4 AI personalization
  • C5 Social & community features
  • C6 Direct-to-consumer
  • C7 Clinician-partnered distribution
  • C8 Institutional ATL outreach (brand / B2B / awareness)
  • C9 TTL data & insight loop (through-the-line data → product improvement)

Reading the matrix: every slide-7 capability has a home in the product across web + apps. The "Status to verify" column is where POs focus — these are the items that turn a designed capability into a *delivered* one (mostly backend, content, and the admin/B2B surfaces from the Platform Backlog).

PO verification process (suggested)

  1. Walk each workflow; confirm the intent matches business goals.
  2. Mark each BR per surface (✅/⚠️/❌) in business-requirements.csv.
  3. For every ❌/⚠️, link to a backlog story (backlog.csv) so it's scheduled.
  4. Confirm the slide-7 matrix shows ✓ for both Mobile and Web before launch sign-off (C7/C8 may be web/admin-only by design).
  5. Re-verify after the Figma signature-screen rebuild and backend wiring.
